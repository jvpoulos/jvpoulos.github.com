Research

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in Data Science in the Department of Health Care Policy at Harvard Medical School.

After receiving my PhD from UC Berkeley in 2019, I held a joint postdoctoral appointment in the Department of Statistical Science at Duke University and the Statistical and Applied Mathematical Sciences Institute (SAMSI), where I participated in the Causal Inference program.

My research focuses on leveraging machine learning for improving causal inference, specifically for counterfactual prediction in observational settings in the social sciences and health data sciences.
