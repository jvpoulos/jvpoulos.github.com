In May 2019, I received a PhD in Political Science with a Designated Emphasis in Computational Science and Engineering from the University of California, Berkeley.

I am a Postdoctoral Associate at the Department of Statistical Science at Duke University and the Statistical and Applied Mathematical Sciences Institute (SAMSI), where I am participating in the Programs on Deep Learning and Causal Inference.

My research focuses on developing methods that leverage machine learning for causal inference, with applications to public policy problems.
