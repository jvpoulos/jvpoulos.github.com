Hello.

I am a PhD Candidate in Political Science (with a Designated Emphasis in Computational and Data Science and Engineering) at UC Berkeley, a NSF Graduate Research Fellow, and a Berkeley Empirical Legal Studies (BELS) Graduate Fellow.

My dissertation research investigates the impact of Reconstruction-era amnesty policy on the behavior of elites and Reconstruction outcomes in the postbellum U.S. South. I am also interested applying Computer Vision and deep learning methods to transcribe handwritten information in historical documents.
